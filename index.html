I served in the military for 8 years prior to transitioning into technology. From traveling the world, to jumping out of airplanes, it has been quite a journey. I have always been curious about computers. In 2020, I discovered a coding bootcamp for veterans called 'Code Platoon' and it has been my passion ever since. I enjoy learning new languages and frameworks and solving problems. I look forward to taking my programming to the next level as I attend a Master of Science in Analytics program at Northwestern University this Fall. When I am away from the keyboard I enjoy spending time with my dog or going to the gym.
